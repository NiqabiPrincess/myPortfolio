About Me

I’m currently a first-year student at Humber, pursuing an advanced diploma in Computer Programming and Analysis. My passion for computer science has been a driving force in my academic journey, and I’ve enjoyed exploring it further through various experiences.

My current goals are to complete my advanced diploma, become more fluent in coding and, try making some more advanced projects.

In high school, I had the opportunity to take several computer science courses, which laid a solid foundation for my programming skills. I also participated in the Canadian Computing Competition (CCC) in 2019 and completed a few online coding courses, all of which have greatly enhanced my understanding of the field. Some of the coding languages I know are; Python, Java, HTML, CSS and, JavaScript.

Beyond my studies, I have a diverse range of interests, including makeup, baking, cooking, knitting, and enjoying nature walks. I’m excited to continue developing my programming abilities and deepening my knowledge of computer science. I look forward to connecting with others who share my passions and interests!
